WHO ARE WE

AVTAR MINHAS

Founder & Owner

GSA Construction Limited.

GSA Construction is a relatively fast growing company in the Fraser Valley providing expert services for your home renovations. We are experts in the market with our key principles being Quality, Experience, Trust, Flexibility. We constantly strive in providing high valued quality services to our customers. Being highly flexible and adaptable we easily accomodate to our customer needs. With our vast experience in home renovations we assure to provide satisfactory results to our customers.

Avtar Minhas, Founder of GSA Construction has nearly 15 years of experience in renovations. His sincerity towards his work, quality workmanship, and adherence to customer care has helped him build a strong relationship with his customers. He and his crew specialze in providing excellent remodelling of your home.

Living by the goals of Trust & Transparency, we are ready to meet you and share our ideas that are suitable for renovating your home !!!
